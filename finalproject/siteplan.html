Cute & Covered Website Plan

Site Name

Cute & Covered

The site name reflects the store's mission to provide fashionable yet modest clothing for women who prioritize modesty. The name emphasizes both the "cute" and "covered" aspects of the clothing offered.

Site Purpose

Cute & Covered aims to provide a comprehensive online shopping experience for women seeking modest clothing that aligns with the dressing standards of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. The site will showcase various types of clothing, including shirts, pants, dresses, swimwear, and deals, simplifying the search for modest fashion.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest deals on modest clothing? Users should be able to view current promotions and discounts on clothing items.
  • How can I sign up for new product updates? Users should be able to sign up for a newsletter to receive updates about new arrivals and promotions.

Color Schema

Pink (#FFC0CB):

  • Background color for the header, footer, and buttons.
  • Accents and hover effects.

White (#FFFFFF):

  • Background color for content sections, item cards, and text.
  • Ensures readability and contrast with the pink accents.

Typography

Font 1: 'Ms Madi'

  • Used for headings, shop name, and buttons.

Font 2: 'Arial', sans-serif

  • Used for body text and general content to provide a clean and readable appearance.
